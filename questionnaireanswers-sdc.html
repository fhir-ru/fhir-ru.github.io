Structured Data Capture Data Capture

Scope and Usage

This profile sets expectations for use of the QuestionnaireAnswers resource within the Structured Data Capture project. This includes identifying which core elements and extensions must be supported.

For the purposes of this profile, Supported means that clients SHALL be capable of processing the element/extension and use the information to control the display and information capture associated with the QuestionnaireAnswers resource. It means that servers SHALL be capable of persisting those elements and returning them in response to requests.

Boundaries and Relationships

This profile is related to the SDC Questionnaire Profile which defines the characteristic of a form "design". This profile focuses on the constraints appropriate to capturing the "answer(s)" to a pre-defined form.

This profile was published on Mon, Apr 21, 2014 00:00+0000 as a draft by U.S. Office of the National Coordinator (ONC) , and profiles the QuestionaireAnswers Resource

The id of this profile is questionnaireanswers-sdc
